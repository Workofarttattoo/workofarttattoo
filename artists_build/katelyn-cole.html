Frequently Asked Questions

How do I prepare for an Ear Curation consultation?

Simply book a "Curation Consultation" with Katelyn. Bring photos of ear styles you like, but keep an open mind—Katelyn will map your specific anatomy to see what placements will heal best and look most harmonious for you.

What jewelry materials does Katelyn use?

We exclusively use implant-grade titanium or solid 14k/18k gold. We never use "surgical steel" or plated metals, as these often contain nickel and can cause irritation or rejection during the healing process.

Does Katelyn perform fine line tattoos on the same day as piercings?

It depends on the complexity. Small fine-line pieces can often be scheduled alongside a piercing appointment. However, for larger tattoo projects, we recommend separate sessions to ensure the body isn't overwhelmed during the initial healing phase.

What is the healing time for a cartilage piercing?

Cartilage piercings generally take 6 to 12 months to fully mature. Katelyn provides a detailed aftercare kit and schedule to ensure your new piece heals perfectly without bumps or irritation.

Can I bring my own jewelry for a new piercing?

To guarantee sterilization and material safety, we only pierce with jewelry purchased from our studio. We can, however, help you transition to your own high-quality heirloom pieces once the piercing is fully healed.
